Louvre Museum

I visited in the summer of 2022

It's a fantastic place. You have to come here. If you dare to come, perhaps you should hire a tourist guide so that you can better understand the history of the place. It is a complex with yellow walls and very well maintained. It is a historical site and famous among tourists. It contains different gadgets with which time could be measured in different eras of Indian history. The price to enter here is ₹15 (about $4.12 Mexican pesos) for Indian residents, and for the general public ₹225-250 (about $61.84 Mexican pesos). It is a good place to visit. I had an amazing experience at the observatory and checked the accuracy of the astronomical instruments that were built over 300 years ago.
